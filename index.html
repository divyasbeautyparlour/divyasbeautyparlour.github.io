Our Story

Beauty Is Our
Passion

At Sree Divya's Beauty Parlour, we believe every woman deserves to feel her most radiant self. Nestled in the heart of Gandhinagar, Kavadiguda, Hyderabad — our boutique parlour is a sanctuary of elegance, skill, and warmth.

Run by the beloved Rekha Mam (Chitra Rekha) — praised by every client for her patience, humility, and skill — we've been a trusted name for over 30 years. From everyday haircuts to dream bridal looks, she treats every client like family.

From perfect eyebrows and haircuts to stunning bridal packages — we bring world-class beauty with pocket-friendly pricing, because you deserve both.
